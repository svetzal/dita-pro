Welcome to the Durham I.T. Professionals Group

The Durham I.T. Professionals group is a peer networking group for I.T. professionals who live or work in Durham Region.

We are in the process of planning our events for 2012, and re-vamping our website. Check back in the coming months for big changes!

This space has been donated by the now-defunkt Durham I.T. Association. Our hope is to re-develop what was the most successful aspect of "DITA" which was the peer networking group.
